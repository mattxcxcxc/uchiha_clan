Uchiha Clan

Sasuke Uchiha

He is a very skilled fighter and has the potential to one day become a powerful ninja. He graduated with the highest marks from the Ninja Academy to become a Genin and is the #1 rookie in the Chunnin exam. His sensei is Hatake Kakashi, and he is teamed up with Uzumaki Naruto, and Haruno Sakura.

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi is a member of the Uchiha Clan from the village of Konohagakure and the elder brother of Sasuke Uchiha. He grew up as the child prodigy of the Uchiha clan, establishing milestones for not only Sasuke, but also for future Uchiha to live up to.

Madara Uchiha

Madara Uchiha is a fictional character from the manga series Naruto, which depicts a world of ninjas. One of the main villains of the Naruto series (Part II), he is one of the co-founders of the Hidden Leaf Village along with friend/rival Hashirama Senju, the First Hokage.
